Welcome to University Police

The overall safety of Northwest requires the active cooperation, assistance and support of the entire campus community. University Police serves to maintain a safe campus environment and discourage inappropriate activity.

Visitor Parking

We welcome all visitors to campus and request that all faculty, staff and students who are receiving guests on campus please make arrangements to obtain a free Visitor parking pass for your guest(s) before they arrive on campus. Guests are defined as non-students, non-faculty/staff. Visitor passes available: in person at University Police, open 24/7 via email by contacting University Police at police@nwmissouri.edu.
